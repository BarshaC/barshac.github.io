As a part of final project, we worked on creating this IOS Mobile Application.
IFriends is a mobile app designed to help international students build connections, make new friends, and integrate into campus life. With a vibrant community and engaging features, IFriends fosters meaningful relationships and supports students as they navigate the challenges of studying abroad.

IFriends is designed to cater to the diverse needs of international students studying at universities around the world.

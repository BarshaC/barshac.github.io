<p>This application serves as a robust clone of Twitter, enabling users to express themselves and interact through a dynamic social platform. Users can compose and post updates, or "tweets," which are then visible on their personal feed as well as on the feeds of users who follow them. The app features a real-time updating system, allowing users to refresh their feed to see the latest posts from others. </p>
<p>
Additionally, the application supports interactive features such as replying to tweets and commenting on posts made by others. This encourages a lively and engaging user experience, fostering community and discussion among participants. Whether sharing thoughts, news, or responding to others, this clone provides a comprehensive platform for digital social interaction.</p>
